Dungeon Crawler Carl is a LitRPG series, a genre that blends science fiction and fantasy with the mechanics of role-playing games. The premise is both absurd and completely compelling. The entire surface of Earth has been destroyed, and the surviving humans have been dropped into a massive underground dungeon system run by an alien civilization that broadcasts the chaos as entertainment to the rest of the galaxy. The main character is Carl, an ordinary man navigating this nightmare alongside his ex-girlfriend's cat, Princess Donut. That combination of high-stakes survival horror and genuinely funny character writing is exactly what has made this series explode in popularity.
