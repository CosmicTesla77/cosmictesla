# GTA6: Release Date, Trailer Leaks, and Everything We Know Right Now
2026-05-16

Grand Theft Auto 6 is the most anticipated video game in history. That is not hyperbole. The original announcement trailer released in December 2023 became the most viewed gaming trailer of all time within 24 hours, accumulating tens of millions of views before most people had finished their morning coffee. Years later, with the release window approaching, search interest in GTA6 has not slowed down. It has accelerated.

Rockstar Games has confirmed GTA6 is set in a fictional version of Miami and South Florida called Vice City, returning to the location of Grand Theft Auto Vice City from 2002 but built from scratch with modern technology. The trailer revealed a female protagonist named Lucia, making GTA6 the first mainline entry in the series to feature a playable female lead. The game also appears to feature a male protagonist, with the two characters seemingly operating as a pair in a story that draws comparisons to Bonnie and Clyde.

The visual fidelity shown in official materials has been genuinely shocking. Rockstar has spent over a decade building the engine that powers GTA6, and what has been shown publicly suggests a level of environmental detail, character animation, and world density that no open world game has previously achieved. Puddles reflect accurate light. Crowds of NPCs behave with individual logic. Wildlife moves through ecosystems. The gap between GTA6 and anything currently available appears significant.

YouTube trending today is surfacing content about GTA6 trailer leaks and new information, which tells you the community is actively hunting for any signal about what comes next. Rockstar is famously secretive about release dates and game details, which creates a vacuum that speculation, leaks, and analysis videos fill constantly.

What is confirmed: the game exists, it is set in Vice City, it has been in development for over a decade with a reported budget exceeding $2 billion, and Rockstar has not announced a final release date as of this writing. What is speculated: various window estimates have circulated, with the gaming community watching Rockstar's communications for any hint of timing.

For context on why this matters beyond gaming: GTA5 released in 2013, generated over $8 billion in revenue, and is still actively played and monetized today. GTA6 does not need to be merely a good game. It needs to be the event of the decade for an industry that has been waiting for it since before some of its youngest players were born. The pressure on Rockstar is unlike anything in entertainment history.
