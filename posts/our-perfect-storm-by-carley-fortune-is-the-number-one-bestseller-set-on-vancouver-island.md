Our Perfect Storm by Carley Fortune Is the Number One Bestseller Set on Vancouver Island
June 24 2026

Our Perfect Storm is Carley Fortune's fifth novel and an instant number one New York Times bestseller, published on May 5 2026 by Berkley. It is a friends to lovers romance following two lifelong best friends, Frankie and George, set against the rainforests and storm tossed beaches of Tofino on Vancouver Island.

## What is Our Perfect Storm about?

Frankie and George have been inseparable since they were eight years old, when George moved in next door with his grandmother. They are both passionate, impulsive, and stubborn, a combination that has them clashing and reconciling for more than two decades while staying strictly friends. The plot turns on a wedding gone wrong. Frankie's fiancé dumps her the morning after the rehearsal dinner, leaving only a note, and George convinces her to take him on the Vancouver Island honeymoon instead. One week in paradise forces the question of whether their friendship can survive becoming something more.

What lifts the book above its premise is how much it cares about the years before that honeymoon. Fortune builds the relationship through a childhood of secrets, scribbled letters, and shared abandonment issues, so the romance reads as the payoff of a long friendship rather than a sudden spark. Reviewers repeatedly described the result as a story about healing as much as falling in love, anchored by family and a strong sense of place.

## Why is Carley Fortune resonating with so many readers?

Because she has turned a reliable formula and a Canadian setting into a brand. Fortune is a former journalist whose books have sold more than four million copies and been translated into 30 languages, with a television adaptation of Every Summer After already in production. Our Perfect Storm is her first title released in hardcover, a signal of how far her commercial profile has climbed. Kirkus gave it a starred review, and the slow burn friends to lovers arc she perfected in Every Summer After is the exact engine readers come back for each summer.

The setting does heavy lifting too. Fortune has made a habit of casting Canadian locations as romantic destinations, and Tofino, with its mist draped beaches, rainforests, and migrating whales, becomes almost a second protagonist. Readers describe finishing the book and immediately looking up flights, which is the kind of immersive escapism that has made her a fixture on the bestseller list.

## Who is this book perfect for?

Readers who love a slow burn friends to lovers arc and an emotional, character driven romance will find exactly what they want. It suits fans of forced proximity, travel romance, and stories that balance swooning with genuine feeling about family and grief. Anyone who tore through Every Summer After or Meet Me at the Lake is the core audience.

If you want a summer romance with real emotional weight and a setting you will not stop thinking about, Our Perfect Storm is among the strongest entries Fortune has written.
