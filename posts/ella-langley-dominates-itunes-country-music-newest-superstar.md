# Ella Langley Dominates iTunes: Who Is Country Music's Newest Superstar
2026-05-20

If you have not heard of Ella Langley yet, you are about to. Right now she holds three of the top six spots on the Apple iTunes Top Songs chart in the United States simultaneously, with Choosin Texas at number one, Be Her at number two, and you look like you love me at number six. Occupying three positions in the top ten of any major chart at the same time is an extraordinary achievement that puts her in the company of very few artists in recent memory.

Ella Langley is a Nashville based country artist who has been building momentum steadily, but the kind of chart dominance she is showing right now signals a breakthrough moment. When an artist has multiple songs charting simultaneously at this level, it means listeners are not just enjoying one hit. They are going deep into the catalog and finding multiple songs worth returning to repeatedly. That is the mark of a genuine artist connection rather than a viral flash in the pan.

Country music has been experiencing a broader mainstream crossover moment, with artists bridging the gap between traditional country sounds and contemporary production in ways that attract audiences who would not have called themselves country fans a few years ago. Langley appears to be riding that wave while also developing a distinct identity within the genre.

For music fans who track emerging artists, Ella Langley is the name to know right now. Three simultaneous top ten iTunes hits is not luck. It is the result of an artist finding her audience and that audience responding in a significant way. Watch for her presence on awards show ballots and festival lineups in the months ahead because this kind of chart performance does not go unnoticed by the industry.
