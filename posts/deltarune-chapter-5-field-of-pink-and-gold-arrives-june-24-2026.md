Deltarune Chapter 5 Field of Pink and Gold Arrives June 24 2026
June 14 2026

Deltarune Chapter 5, subtitled The Field of Pink and Gold, releases on June 24 2026 at 11:00 AM Eastern time as a free update across Nintendo Switch, Switch 2, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, PC, and Mac. Creator Toby Fox confirmed the date during the June 9 2026 Nintendo Direct, and the trailer closed with a single line, Chapter 6 is waiting, teasing the next installment of his planned seven chapter saga. Anyone who already owns the game gets the new chapter automatically at no cost.

## Do you have to pay for Deltarune Chapter 5?

No. If you already own Deltarune on any supported platform, Chapter 5 downloads as a free update the moment it goes live. Toby Fox committed to this structure back in April 2025, promising he would only ever charge for content that already existed and would add everything completed afterward at no additional charge. The full game currently sells for $24.99 and covers Chapters 1 through 4. After June 24, that same one time price includes all five released chapters, which makes it one of the better values in modern role playing games.

## How does Deltarune connect to Undertale?

Deltarune is a standalone parallel story set in a different continuity from Undertale, Toby Fox's 2015 indie phenomenon. You do not need to have finished Undertale to play it, though longtime fans will recognize familiar faces, musical motifs, and the same razor sharp blend of humor, heart, and dread. The story follows a teenager named Kris, their classmate Susie, and a gentle prince named Ralsei as they move through strange Dark Worlds, with the larger mystery unfolding slowly across each chapter. Fox writes, composes, and directs the project largely himself, which is part of why the gaps between releases have been so long.

## Why is the short wait for Chapter 5 surprising?

Because Deltarune has historically made fans wait years. Chapter 1 arrived on Halloween 2018, Chapter 2 followed in September 2021, and Chapters 3 and 4 launched together on June 5 2025. Getting Chapter 5 barely a year later, with trailer music by Carlos insaneintherainmusic Eiene, represents the shortest gap between installments the series has ever seen. The quicker cadence, plus the explicit Chapter 6 tease, suggests Fox is closing in on the finish line of a project he has been building for nearly a decade.

The floral subtitle, The Field of Pink and Gold, hints at a brighter, more pastoral chapter, a deliberate contrast to the dark clouds Fox referenced at the close of Chapter 4. In his release newsletter he framed the moment as turning around to watch the sun before it sets completely, language fans have spent days parsing for clues. With this series, the marketing copy is rarely an accident.

For a game that has cultivated one of the most devoted fan bases in independent gaming, a concrete release date with only days to wait is a rare gift. Deltarune has always traded in mystery, emotion, and the sense that something larger is moving just out of view, and Chapter 5 promises to deepen that feeling while pushing the overarching story toward its endgame. If you have been holding off, picking up the $24.99 base game before June 24 means you walk straight into five chapters of one of the most singular role playing games ever made.
