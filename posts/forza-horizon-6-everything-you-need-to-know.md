## What Sets the Horizon Series Apart?

The Horizon series sets itself apart from simulation-focused racing games by prioritizing fun and accessibility without sacrificing the depth that car enthusiasts crave. Players can jump into a hypercar and immediately feel the rush of speed across gorgeous open world environments while the game rewards those who put in the time to master its systems with increasingly refined control and performance.

## Why Does Forza Horizon World Design Matter?

What makes Forza Horizon titles enduring is their world design. Each entry has been set in a different real world inspired location with dynamic weather, day and night cycles, and a living environment that makes exploration as rewarding as racing. The combination of hundreds of meticulously modeled cars and a world that begs to be driven through at full speed is a formula that has built one of gaming's most loyal fanbases.
