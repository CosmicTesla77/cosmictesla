## How Does the LEGO Game Formula Work?

The LEGO game series has always operated on a simple formula that works precisely because it never overcomplicates itself. Take a beloved franchise, rebuild its most iconic characters and settings out of virtual LEGO bricks, fill the experience with collectibles and light puzzle-solving, and wrap it in a humor that lands for both children and the adults playing alongside them. The Batman property has been one of the strongest fits for that formula since the original LEGO Batman launched in 2008, and Legacy of the Dark Knight appears to be the most ambitious entry in that lineage.
