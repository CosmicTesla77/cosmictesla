About CosmicTesla

CosmicTesla is a real-time trending search tracker that surfaces the most popular topics being searched across the United States right now. No noise, no filler — just the top 20 trending searches, updated throughout the day.

Trend data is sourced directly from the Google Trends RSS feed and sorted by search volume so you always see what's actually capturing people's attention.

Each trend card shows the approximate search volume, a relevant news headline, and a link to explore the topic further on Google. The page is lightweight by design — no accounts, no tracking, no clutter.

CosmicTesla is an independent project built for anyone who wants a quick, clean pulse on what the internet is talking about today.
