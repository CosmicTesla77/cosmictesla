Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Overview

CosmicTesla is a simple, read-only trending search tracker. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, or any form of user tracking. We do not collect your IP address, browsing behavior, or any personally identifiable information. No account or login is required to use this site.

Third-Party Data Sources

Trending data is fetched from the Google Trends public RSS feed on your behalf when you load the page. Your browser does not communicate directly with Google Trends — the request is made server-side. Google's own privacy policy governs their services.

Trend cards may link to external news articles and Google Search. Clicking those links will take you to third-party sites, which have their own privacy practices we do not control.

Hosting

CosmicTesla is hosted on third-party infrastructure. Standard server-side access logs (IP address, timestamp, requested URL) may be retained by our hosting provider for a limited period for security and operational purposes. We do not use these logs to identify or profile users.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.
